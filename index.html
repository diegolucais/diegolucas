Hello, I’m Diego.

About Me

Diego Lucas has always felt a deep connection to the ancient world, with a particular fascination for Egypt. Inspired by Tomb Raider and The Mummy (1999), he once dreamt of becoming an archaeologist. However, upon realising that books allowed him to create his own expeditions, he chose the path of writing instead.

From an early age, Diego nurtured a passion for reading, which soon evolved into a desire to tell his own stories. At the age of 12, he created his first character, Alex Canyon, giving life to The Scarabs of Seth – the novel that marked the beginning of his journey as a writer.

My Books

The Scarabs of Seth

My debut novel. Alex Canyon discovers a hidden temple and a deadly secret: three ancient scarabs of unimaginable power. As rivals close in, he’s swept into a global race against time and myth.

The Wound Shattering

Upcoming. Khyan Darkwinter wakes on the petrified shore of a world abandoned by its gods. Hunted by a monstrous beast, he must survive long enough to uncover who he is – and why he was brought here.

The Mystic Staff of Ra

Sequel to The Scarabs of Seth. Alex holds a fragment of Ra’s legendary staff – but to stop Seth from returning in full power, he must find the lost city of the sun god and reunite the pieces.
