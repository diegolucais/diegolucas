About Me

Born on 28 February 1994 in Taubaté, São Paulo, Brazil, Diego Lucas has always felt a deep connection to the ancient world, with a particular fascination for Egypt. Inspired by Tomb Raider and The Mummy (1999), he once dreamt of becoming an archaeologist. However, upon realising that books allowed him to create his own expeditions, he chose the path of writing instead.

From an early age, Diego nurtured a passion for reading, which soon evolved into a desire to tell his own stories. At the age of 12, he created his first character, Alex Canyon, giving life to The Scarabs of Seth – the novel that marked the beginning of his journey as a writer.
