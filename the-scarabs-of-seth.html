The Scarabs of Seth

On Bolivia's treacherous Death Road, archaeologist Alex Canyon finds himself searching for a missing friend. During his mission, he uncovers a hidden temple in the mountains, brimming with secrets that surpass modern understanding. Among unexpected Egyptian hieroglyphs and an enigmatic artefact, Alex is confronted with an unimaginable revelation: the key to a forgotten tomb in Egypt.

Driven by an insatiable thirst for the unknown, Alex unravels an ancient scroll that speaks of three legendary scarabs, capable of bestowing devastating powers upon their holder. However, his journey quickly escalates into a contest for the artefacts against Johnny West, a ruthless businessman, and William Wright, the charismatic leader of the mysterious Order of Seth—two rivals prepared to do whatever it takes to achieve their goals.

Embark on this thrilling adventure, where each discovery triggers greater dangers and deeper mysteries. In a plot of intense rivalries and unexpected alliances, Alex faces a world where history and mythology intertwine, challenging the limits of his courage and survival instincts.
